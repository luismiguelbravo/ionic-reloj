Titulo:

Mimuqui

Descripcion breve:

Record events in the past and in the future

Descripcion completa:

Record events in the past and in the future

It is possible to add a future date, and the application will count how much time in years, months, days, hours, minutes and seconds is missing for the event to occur.

By adding a date in the past, the application will calculate how much time has passed in years, months, days, hours, minutes and seconds left before the event occurs.
